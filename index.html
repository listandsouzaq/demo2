We don’t manufacture — we preserve. From the red soils of coastal Karnataka to your kitchen, every seed, oil and spice tells a story of purity.

Beyond Organic: The Shridatt Way

Founded in 1987 by Vittal Poojary, Shridatt began as a small family run ghani (wooden press) unit in Mangalore. Even today, we use the same traditional cold‑pressing method – no heat, no chemicals – ensuring every drop retains its natural antioxidants, flavor and therapeutic properties. Our coconut oil is pressed within hours of harvesting the kernels; our gingelly oil still carries the nutty aroma that reminds your grandmother’s kitchen.

We work directly with 200+ small farmers who practice regenerative agriculture. No pesticides, no synthetic fertilizers – only compost, cow dung and traditional wisdom. Our spices are stone‑ground, our honeys are raw and unpasteurised, and our camphor is naturally derived from resins. In an age of ultra‑processed foods, Shridatt remains stubbornly slow.

"We believe that nature doesn't need improvement — it needs respect." — Poojary Family
